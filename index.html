Recipes In your kitchen

Quinoa Vegetable Salad

Ingredients: Quinoa, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, red onion, cucumber, olive oil, lemon juice, fresh herbs (like parsley or basil), feta cheese (optional). Method: Roast diced vegetables, then mix with cooked quinoa. Toss with olive oil, lemon juice, herbs, and feta cheese if desired.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients: Chicken thighs, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, cumin, paprika, tomato puree, cream, butter, onion, green chilies. Method: Marinate chicken in a mix of yogurt and spices, then grill. Cook onions, add spices and tomato puree, simmer, add cream and grilled chicken.

Vegetable Pad Thai

Ingredients: Rice noodles, tofu or shrimp, bean sprouts, bell peppers, carrots, eggs, peanuts, tamarind paste, soy sauce, lime juice. Method: Stir-fry tofu or shrimp with vegetables and cooked noodles. Add tamarind paste, soy sauce, lime juice, and crushed peanuts.

Miso Glazed Salmon

Ingredients: Salmon fillets, miso paste, soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, sesame oil, ginger, garlic. Method: Mix miso, soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, sesame oil, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Marinate salmon fillets, then bake or grill until cooked through.
